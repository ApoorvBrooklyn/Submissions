technologies and methodologies to identify and diagnose
diseases affecting plants. It utilizes diverse tools, including
sensors, imaging systems, and data analysis, to discern subtle
signs of diseases such as visual symptoms, molecular changes,

facilitates early intervention and targeted management strategies,
contributing to enhanced floral health, preserved yields, and
sustainable agricultural practices.
